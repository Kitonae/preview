How It Works

Five Steps. One Click.

Open your .watch file and the importer takes care of everything — from reading your legacy show to delivering a complete summary of what was brought over.

01

Open

Select your WATCHOUT 6 show file and the importer reads its contents automatically.

02

Analyze

Every element of your show is identified — media, timelines, cues, displays, and settings — including shows from all WATCHOUT 6 versions.

03

Convert

Show elements are translated to their WATCHOUT 7 equivalents, with positions, scales, and timing preserved precisely.

04

Register Media

All your images, videos, audio files, and 3D models are automatically linked to the WATCHOUT 7 media library.

05

Review

A detailed report shows you exactly what was imported, what needs attention, and if any files are missing.

Accuracy

Pixel-Perfect Conversion

WATCHOUT 6 and 7 handle positions, sizes, and values differently. The importer automatically adjusts every detail so your show looks exactly the way you designed it.

  • Positions and depth are remapped to the new coordinate system
  • Anchor points are recalculated to match each image
  • Scale and opacity values are normalized automatically
  • Composition layouts are adjusted to stay relative
  • Projector viewpoints and lens settings carry over
  • Motion paths and curves transfer with full accuracy
  • Layer order and display assignments stay intact

Built to Last

Why It Works

The importer was designed to handle real-world shows of any size and complexity, with reliability you can trust on show day.

🎯 Handles Every Version

Whether your show was created in an early WATCHOUT 6 release or the latest 6.5.1 update, the importer recognizes and converts it correctly.

🔄 Live Progress

Watch the import happen in real time. A progress dialog keeps you informed at every step, and you can cancel at any moment.

🚀 Automatic Media Linking

Your images, videos, audio, and 3D models are automatically registered with the WATCHOUT 7 media library, preserving your original folder structure.

🎲 3D Model Support

3D models with multiple textures are brought over with each texture mapped to the correct surface, ready to use immediately.

📈 Nothing Gets Lost

If something can't be converted, you'll know. The import report clearly lists anything that needs manual attention.

⚡ Fast & Reliable

Even large shows with hundreds of cues, complex timelines, and dozens of displays import quickly and accurately.

Transparency

Know Exactly What Happened

Every import produces a clear, readable report so you always know what was brought over and what might need your attention.

  • Original show name, version, and import date
  • Counts for timelines, cues, media, displays, and variables
  • Clear alerts for any unsupported features
  • Missing file detection so you can locate media quickly
  • Summaries for each area of your show
