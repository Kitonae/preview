WATCHOUT 7
✦ New Workflow

Bring Your Legacy Shows Forward

Import your WATCHOUT 6 shows directly into WATCHOUT 7. Your timelines, cues, media, displays, and stage setup transfer automatically — so you can keep creating instead of rebuilding. The five-step pipeline handles everything from reading the legacy file to delivering a detailed report of what was brought over.

Positions, scales, anchor points, and motion paths are precisely recalculated to match WATCHOUT 7's coordinate system, ensuring your show looks exactly the way you designed it. All WATCHOUT 6 versions are supported.

Comprehensive coverage

Media, cues, compositions, 3D models, animation paths, control inputs (MIDI, DMX, Art-Net), displays, projectors, and warp geometry all convert automatically.

Pixel-perfect conversion

Vertical and depth positions are remapped, anchor points recalculated, and scale values normalized — eliminating manual correction work.

Automatic media registration

All images, videos, audio files, and 3D models are automatically linked to the WATCHOUT 7 media library during import.

Detailed import report

A summary shows exactly what was imported, what needs attention, and whether any source files are missing.
