The New Optimizer

Release 2026.4

A ground-up redesign of the Optimizer brings faster processing, smarter re-optimization workflows, and broader format support. The new engine introduces a "Create Version" workflow that lets you re-optimize existing content with a new output codec without touching the original. Audio handling now supports up to 65,535 channels via .wav files, and corrected color interpretation keeps output faithful across the pipeline.
