The New Optimizer

A ground-up redesign of the Optimizer engine — faster, more flexible, and built for complex production pipelines. Whether you're preparing content for the first time or revisiting it with new delivery requirements, the new Optimizer gives you more control at every step.

The new "Create Version" workflow lets you re-optimize existing assets with a different output codec, generating delivery variants without touching the original. Color interpretation has been corrected across all pipelines, ensuring output faithfully matches source material from ingest to render.

Up to 65,535 audio channels

WAV-based audio optimization now supports the full extended channel range, covering even the most demanding immersive audio setups.

Create Version with new codec

Re-optimize any asset with a fresh output codec — no need to re-ingest the original source file.

Corrected color interpretation

Color values are accurately preserved through the optimization pipeline, eliminating subtle shifts seen in previous versions.

Upgraded dependency stack

Replaced russimp with russimp-ng for improved 3D asset handling and broader format compatibility.
