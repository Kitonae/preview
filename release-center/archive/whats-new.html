★ Major Release

The New Optimizer

A ground-up redesign of the Optimizer brings faster processing, smarter re-optimization workflows, and broader format support. The new engine introduces a "Create Version" workflow that lets you re-optimize existing content with a new output codec — without touching the original. Audio handling has been dramatically expanded, now supporting up to 65,535 channels via .wav files. Color interpretation has also been corrected to ensure output faithfully matches source material across all pipelines.

Released March 9, 2026
