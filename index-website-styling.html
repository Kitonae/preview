Pixel-Perfect conversion

WATCHOUT 6 and 7 handle positions, sizes, and values differently. The importer automatically adjusts every detail so your show looks exactly the way you designed it.

  • Positions and depth are remapped to the new coordinate system
  • Anchor points are recalculated to match each image
  • Scale and opacity values are normalized automatically
  • Composition layouts are adjusted to stay relative
  • Projector viewpoints and lens settings carry over
  • Motion paths and curves transfer with full accuracy
  • Layer order and display assignments stay intact
WATCHOUT 6 WATCHOUT 7
Vertical position 150 → −150
Depth position 80 → −80
Scale 0.75 → 75
Opacity 0.5 → 50
Anchor point 20 → auto
Layer order 3 → 2
MIDI channel 1 → 0
